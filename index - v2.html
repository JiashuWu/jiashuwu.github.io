Projects

Profware Searching Platform |Academic Searching Platform

During my intern at Profware, I conducted research on recommender systems and information retrieval and developed an academic searching platform, which supports searching researchers by keywords, research discipline areas etc. This work advances related works by allowing researchers to be retrieved base on not only the keywords they explicitly mentioned in their publications, but also keywords and research disciplines that are related to them but are not explicitly mentioned, e.g. a machine translation researcher who didn’t explicitly mention the term “computer science” in his/her publications can also be retrieved when user queries computer science. Besides searching, the platform also supports browsing researchers by discipline areas, complement functionalities such as query auto-complete have also been implemented.


Pacman | AI Agent | github.com/JiashuWu/Pacman-Project

An AI agent that can play Pacman Game. It utilizes AI planning techniques including heuristic search, Q-learning etc. Done during Artificial Intelligence subject at University of Melbourne, our agent received full mark.


Neurograph |Android App | jiashuwu.github.io/project_neurograph.html | github.com/JiashuWu/NeuroGraph

Neurograph, a drawing pattern collector which can be used to assist the diagnostics of Alzheimer’s Disease. Built during the Talented Student Program, which is a research program, with dedicated documentation and user manual to support better user experience. I did this through self-learning, reading related academic papers, and discussing with my supervisor Associate Professor Simon Poon. Now the app is publicly available in Google Play Store and Huawei App Store. The project also involves substantial amount of data analysis to statistically analyze the data captured by the app.


Luckydays | Android App | jiashuwu.github.io/project_luckydays.html | github.com/JiashuWu/Luckydays

A note-taking tool combined with remain days counting functionality. The idea came from observing user’s need, as well as my own thinking of some currently available note-taking apps. Done by self-learning Android programming, and discussing on technical forums like Stack Overflow and CSDN etc. Thoroughly tested on devices with various screen sizes and different versions of the Android system. Now the app is available in Samsung Galaxy Store, Huawei, Xiaomi and ALi App stores etc.


Personal Website | Website jiashuwu.github.io/project_personalwebsite.html |Google search David Jiashu Wu

My personal website, which is a place to share my personal information, projects, as well as my CV etc. Done during spare time of my summer vacation, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.


Strokewise | Website | jiashuwu.github.io/project_strokewise.html | jiashuwu.info1003.github.io

A website aimed to broaden the public awareness towards the Stroke disease, since we do want to use our technological knowledge to benefit the society. Completed during the website design subject at USYD, won the Best Design Award at the end of the semester.


Sudoku Solver |Program| jiashuwu.github.io/project_sudokusolver.html |github.com/JiashuWu/Sudokusolver
